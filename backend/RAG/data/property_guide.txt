# Ali Real Estate — Knowledge Base Guide

## DHA Phase 6 - Residential Overview
DHA Phase 6 is one of the most prestigious residential sectors in Lahore. It features wide carpeted roads, underground electricity, and 24/7 security. The sector is home to the Raya Fairways Club and several international schools. 5 Marla houses in Phase 6 typically feature 3 bedrooms and designer kitchens. Possession is fully available for all blocks.

## Bahria Town Karachi - Investment Guide
Bahria Town Karachi offers a luxury lifestyle with its own theme park, hospital, and replicas of world landmarks like the Eiffel Tower and Taj Mahal. It is a gated community with advanced security. 1 Kanal plots in Bahria are ideal for high-end villas. The precinct system ensures that commercial and residential areas are well-separated.

## Legal and Tax Information
Property transactions in Pakistan are subject to several taxes:
1. Capital Value Tax (CVT): Typically 2% of the registered value.
2. Stamp Duty: 3% of the total value paid at the time of transfer.
3. FBR Withholding Tax: Depends on your status as a Filer or Non-Filer (lower for Filers).
The transfer process usually involves obtaining an NDC (No Demand Certificate) from the society office.

## Property Maintenance & Possession
Possession of a plot allows the owner to start construction after the society approves the architectural map. Maintenance charges are paid monthly and cover security, horticulture, and garbage collection. Solar panel installations are permitted and encouraged in newer phases to promote green living.
